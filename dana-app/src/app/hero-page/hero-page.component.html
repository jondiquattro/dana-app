My Passion

To share, teach and encourage women in their health pursuits. Often we find ourselves in a frustrating cycle of on again off again with dieting, never really getting anywhere and leaving us frustrated, tired, and often feeling hopeless.

I am here to tell you there is HOPE!

My name is Dana Michelle, and I, too, was caught up in a vicious cycle of defeat. I didn’t know how to eat, what to eat, or how to accomplish my health and weight goals. I tried fad diets, high intensity interval training, weight lifting, cardio, starving myself, eating dry chicken, brown rice, and broccoli with fake fat-free butter spray. I tried it all. But as I grew in knowledge and learned what healthy eating ACTUALLY was, I learned that I can accomplish my goals, eat healthy, nourishing and satisfying meals, and ENJOY food, which is very important to me.

BEING Healthy, not just EATING HEALTHY is the Key!

I used to want to just LOOK good. I wasn’t really thinking about actually taking care of myself. I certainly wasn’t thinking about the metabolic damage I was doing by my yo-yo diet extremist ways. But as I aged, I noticed that I forced my body to “look good” while causing damage to myself. It was very difficult to maintain and I felt awful. To maintain the weight loss, I exercised myself to death and ate foods that not only tasted terrible, but barely kept me full or satisfied. I was hungry all the time! I could only keep up these diet and exercise routines for so long, and then I would CRASH! Then I felt ashamed, AGAIN and I was SO TIRED of going around and around the same mountain. I knew God had more for me than to be in a constant state of thinking about my weight and how I looked- going from highs to lows and constantly fixating on myself. I was SICK of thinking about MYSELF all the time. Can you relate?

Then God put a friend in my life who taught me to start thinking about food as nourishment and about my overall health, not just how I looked, or if I was skinny enough, if my hips were thin enough, or if I weighed what a woman at 5’10 “should” weigh. She pointed me to research about how food nourished and healed my body, and how if I ate wholesome foods, then my skin, nails, hair, and even my brain would all function and flourish the way God designed it to, and my body would shed unhealthy weight. Yes, I said God. He is a HUGE part of this journey! I started my journey looking into what God intended food to do

for us and I started to fall in love with what God made, HOW and WHY He made it, and WHAT it does for us in the most amazing ways! My foodie taste buds were OVERJOYED with what I was cooking up in the kitchen as it was no longer plain, dry, low fat, and just plain gross franken-foods. I felt better; satisfied, satiated, I…FELT…SO…GOOD! All the things improved-skin, hair, mood, ENERGY, I could go on and on. And, I lost weight without being miserable! It’s funny how getting back to what God intended for us changes things so drastically! My family was much happier with mealtime as well!

For the first time, I saw food for what it was: delicious abundance that God gave us so we can have healthy, nourished and supported bodies. Completely satisfying. Wholesome and unaltered by man. Looking for a health restoring miracle? Eat Gods Good Foods!!!

I found my FOOD FREEDOM.

Praise God, my MIND started to transform. I was no longer looking at myself with shame and disgust. I saw my body in a whole new way-as the temple of the Holy Spirit.

I desire to help you find this as well. Let’s start this journey together, TODAY!!!!!

Her Story...

I started this process as an exhausted mom of three. I tried to eat right and do all the healthy things but needed a nap daily(not that I actually got one, lol) to the point I would fall asleep driving or reading to the kids.

I was gaining weight even with my efforts! Like a couple pounds every month gaining weight. My PCOS and hormones were out of control. I had lost weight before but my methods weren't working.

By week 2 with this program I had more energy and felt better.

I didn't take supplements, and I was never hungry. The biggest thing was deciding to take care of myself. And then learning how God intended me to do that. I don't have time with my 3 littles to do things by myself often, but my little family can take a walk or make soup. I did planks and push ups while homeschooling. Reading lessons in line, and the audio book while driving kids to the doctor. Honestly, I couldn't safely continue how I was living before I started Dana's program. And I figured I can do anything I need to for a short period of time while learning.

Dana and this program made it easy. Step by step and day by day I learned a little bit. The more I made these choices, the more time and energy I had. Now I can run and play with my kids!! Since meals are planned in advance we have time for board games. My kids are learning what kinds of foods God made for us and changing their lives as well.

God sent Dana to me! Dana offered so much support. She uses scientific studies, down to earth explanations, and Bible verses to inform and support you in being the best you can be. I ended my program climbing uphill 1.3 miles to a waterfall while wearing my 2.5 yr old daughter! Never getting tired or out of breath. All I could think about was that not only can I make it through my day without falling asleep, but I now have the energy to play and enjoy my family!! I can't wait to see how this program continues to improve my life and journey over the next couple years.
Jennifer
